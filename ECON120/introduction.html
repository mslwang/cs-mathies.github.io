Chapter 1: Economic Issues and Concepts

Definitions:

factors of production: resources used to produce goods and services; land, labour, capital

goods: tangible products

services: intangible products

production: act of making goods or services

consumption: act of using goods or services

opportunity cost: value of next best alternative that is forgone when one alternative is chosen

barter: system in which goods and services are traded directly for other goods and services

traditional economy: economy is which behaviour is based mostly on tradition

command economy: economy in which most economic decisions are made by a central planning authority

free-market economy: economy in which most economic decisions are made by private households and firms

mixed economy: economy in which economic decisions are made by firms and households and some by the government

Chapter 2: Economic Theories, Data, and Graphs

Definitions:

normative statement: statement about what ought to be; based on a value judgement

positive statement: statement about what actually is, was, or will be; not based on judgement
